Menus

How you choose to eat affects the whole food system. Our old town restaurant menu reflects the Virginia growing season and features the best hand-fed, farm-raised, organically grown bounty that our region has to offer. We support humane, sustainable and responsible farming practices for the simple fact: local food, raised and produced by people who care, is better. Our menus change daily and reflect the season.
